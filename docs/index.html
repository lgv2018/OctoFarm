OctoFarm Version 1.1.5

Web server and client combo for managing and monitoring multiple Octoprint instances.
Created and maintained by James Mackay from NotExpectedYet

What is OctoFarm?

OctoFarm is a web server and client combo for unifying multiple instances of Octoprint. You can manage and monitor as many instances as you want from a single interface giving you full control over your 3D printer farm.

Management

Manage and control every aspect of your octoprint run farm.

Monitoring

With a selection of views that enable full farm monitoring.

History

Keep track of historical successful, failed or cancelled prints.

Current Features

100% Free and Open Source

All source code is available to check out and you get the entire feature set for free!

Dashboard

Get a better insight into your farm and current metrics whilst giving you a quick access to individual printer controls.

Monitoring

Multiple views to monitor your farm. You can manage your printers and keep an eye on everything at once.

File Manager

Quickly switch between multiple printers and manage the files on them.

History

Get a historical look over your farm by keeping notes and tracking success/cancelled/failure rates.

Filament

Log your types and colours into the database, set a default colour for a printer and keep a log in history. inc. Filament Manager Plugin support

Coming Soon!

User controls and permissions

Give your users permissions to only do certain things on your farm.

Job/Queue system

Add in and queue up job lists for upcoming projects.

Statistics Page

Pull up your farms statistics by any metric you like with fancy graphs

Online Version

Full online version with no setup required, just install an OctoPrint plugin, add your printers and go.

Multiple Nozzle Support

See the temps from all of your nozzles instead of just a single one.

Heated Chamber Support

Check out your heated chamber temperature readings.
